Abstract


Today, Modern Technology is taking over the field of education, serving as an essential teaching tool for facilitating student knowledge acquisition. It is found that students, especially young learners, respond more effectively to picture-based books and actual physical objects for their study instead of traditional books that are primarily textual. This is very beneficial for teachers and parents in developing studentâ€™s linguistic skills. In this paper, an application of image-to-text and speech-based learning aid for young children using a deep learning approach is presented. With the help of an android application, the image of an object can be captured and uploaded to Google Firebase, which acts as the primary Cloud Service in this application.

The image can then be processed and identified using a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN). The text and speech corresponding to the recognized image are then outputted to the corresponding user. The application aims to revolutionize learning aids for young children, especially in pandemic-like scenarios and make them more effective and intuitive.
